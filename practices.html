Practices

At Tobarrak Law Chamber, we are committed to providing exceptional legal services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. We offer comprehensive legal solutions across various practice areas founded on principles of integrity and professionalism.

Corporate Law

Assisting businesses of all sizes in navigating the complex legal landscape. From entity formation and governance to mergers and acquisitions, contract drafting, and compliance matters.

Commercial Litigation

Protecting your interests in breach of contract, shareholder disputes, or intellectual property conflicts. We advocate zealously for our clients both inside and outside the courtroom.

Real Estate Law

Whether you're buying, selling, leasing, or dealing with property disputes, we provide comprehensive legal counsel to ensure your interests are safeguarded at every step.

Family Law

Compassionate guidance and legal representation for divorce, child custody, adoption, and prenuptial agreements to help clients navigate challenging familial issues with dignity.

Intellectual Property

Securing patents, trademarks, and copyrights, as well as enforcing rights against infringement and defending against allegations of intellectual property violations.

Criminal Law

Strong advocacy for individuals facing criminal charges, ensuring that their rights are protected and advocating for the best possible outcome.

Employment Law

Protecting the rights of both employees and employers. From drafting employment contracts and policies to resolving disputes and litigating employment-related claims.

Banking and Finance

Strategic counsel to financial institutions, businesses, and individuals regarding regulatory compliance, loan transactions, or debt restructuring.

Environmental Law

Navigating regulatory compliance, land use matters, and environmental litigation to achieve sustainable and environmentally responsible outcomes.

Company Law

Comprehensive legal guidance in matters pertaining to corporate governance, compliance, mergers and acquisitions, and contract negotiation for domestic and international businesses.

General Legal Services

Civil litigation, criminal defense, family law, immigration law, and alternative dispute resolution tailored to the unique needs of each client.

Property Management for NRBs

Specialized services for non-resident Bangladeshis for seamless property transactions, investments, acquisitions, lease agreements, and title verification.
